### Overview



##### Built an Advanced Excel MIS Dashboard to monitor sales performance, employee achievements, incentive payouts, regional revenue distribution, and product category performance.



### Key Features

* ##### KPI Reporting Dashboard
* ##### Employee Revenue Ranking
* ##### Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
* ##### Product Category Analysis
* ##### Region-wise Revenue Tracking
* ##### Dynamic Slicers and Filters
* ##### Automated Incentive Calculation
